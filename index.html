    tutorial

    In the khoima clicker window, click the khoima in order to earn gamergoo.

    When you have enough gamergoo, you can buy buildings inside the shop.

    After you buy buildings from the shop, they will appear in the buildings window.

    You can view the recent patchnotes in the changelog, or watch YouTube videos in firekhoi.

    Some buildings like khoisweeper unlock minigames that can be accessed by the desktop icons.


    You can also drag around these windows, and use the window control buttons to minimize, maximize, and close the windows.

    Use CTRL+SHIFT+G to reset the window positions back to default.

    Use CTRL+SHIFT+F to switch between Windows XP and 98 themes.
